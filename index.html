Hawaii Quiz App

Aloha! Hawai’i, the land of paradise and home to the most popular vacation destination in the world! Here is a quiz app that has a set of 5 questions and keeps track of your score. Living in Hawaii and being a former tour guide I have always wondered how much people on the mainland and other countries know about Hawai’i. The app features a restart button, a retry button after finishing the quiz and responsive design.

Device Hunt

As a minimalistic tech enthusiast, I always want to find a device that has just enough specs to suit my needs and offers a lot of capabilities for its price. But specs alone do not tell you how good the device actually is. So this app, on top of showing all the specs, will give you a list of review videos of the device you have searched, perfect for those looking for a device suit their needs. Searches can be filtered by phone brand, device, and model. The app uses Fono and Youtube API.

Last Minute Eats

Being a part-time worker and learning to code full-time. I could not afford to spend hours preparing a meal. So I decided why not make an app where people can only view recipes that are short and quick? Last Minute Eats has all the recipes perfect for those in a hurry or just lazy with less than 5 ingredients and 7 steps.

Myusedcarsalesman

I had a very difficult time selling my beloved Mazda 2. Dealing with half-interested buyers, low ballers, and ones that decide to ditch at the last minute. Because I understand all the hassle people go through when selling their used car, I decided to make an app that saves car owner’s time by finding individuals who can sell their car and those who want to make some extra bucks by selling other people’s cars. All the user information on this app is protected with the JWT authentication system.
